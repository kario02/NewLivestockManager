Help Center - Livestock Management System

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register a new animal?
Navigate to the **Animal Management** module and click on **"Register Cow"** to add a new animal.
How can I add vaccination records?
In the **Health Management** section, go to **Vaccination Records** and add a new vaccination entry.
Where can I generate reports?
Reports can be generated under **Reports and Analytics**. You can filter reports based on **Animal Health, Breeding, or Milk Production**.

Module Help

  • **Animal Management** - Register and search for animals.
  • **Breeding Management** - Track breeding and insemination records.
  • **Health Management** - Log treatments and vaccinations.
  • **Milk Production** - Record milk production and manage sales.
  • **Procurement Management** - Manage livestock-related purchases.
  • **Reports & Analytics** - Generate data reports and insights.
